SIMPELE .HTACCESS VOOR NAZORGHULP.NL

Kopieer deze code en vervang je hele .htaccess:

RewriteEngine On

# Redirect /blogs to blogs.html
RewriteRule ^blogs/?$ /blogs.html [L]

# SPA routing - send everything else to index.html
RewriteCond %{REQUEST_FILENAME} !-f
RewriteCond %{REQUEST_FILENAME} !-d
RewriteCond %{REQUEST_URI} !^/blogs.html$
RewriteRule . /index.html [L]

# Browser caching
<IfModule mod_expires.c>
ExpiresActive on
ExpiresByType application/javascript "access plus 1 hour"
ExpiresByType text/css "access plus 1 hour" 
ExpiresByType image/png "access plus 1 day"
</IfModule>

BELANGRIJK: Test na upload met Ctrl+F5!
